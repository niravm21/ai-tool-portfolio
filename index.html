Stock Earnings Scraper

Monitors upcoming earnings releases and delivers a formatted summary email so you never miss a report.

How it works

1
Scrapes upcoming earnings dates for a watchlist of tickers
2
Pulls actual vs expected EPS and revenue figures post-release
3
Formats and sends a digest email on a scheduled cadence

Sample output

SubjectEarnings digest — week of 12 May 2025

CV Screener

Extracts key skills from a job description and scores uploaded CVs against them, ranking candidates automatically.

How it works

1
Parses the job description to extract required and preferred skills
2
Reads each CV and matches skills using keyword and semantic analysis
3
Scores and ranks candidates, flagging matched and missing skills

Sample output

87%
Jane Smith
Senior Data Analyst — 5 yrs exp
Matched skills
Python SQL Tableau Stakeholder comms A/B testing
Missing skills
dbt Looker

Built with

Python spaCy PyMuPDF scikit-learn FastAPI

Project Deadline Emailer

Monitors project tasks and automatically emails stakeholders when deadlines are approaching or overdue.

How it works

1
Reads task data from a spreadsheet or project management source
2
Runs daily and checks each task against today's date
3
Sends a tailored alert email to the assigned owner with priority level

Sample output

Fromdeadlines-bot@yourapp.com
SubjectDaily deadline summary — 12 May 2025
Q2 financial report Due 10 May Overdue
Client proposal draft Due 14 May 2 days
Design review sign-off Due 15 May 3 days
Sprint retrospective doc Due 20 May On track

Built with
